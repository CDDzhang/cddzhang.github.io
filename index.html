My research focuses on UAV visual perception and autonomous navigation, covering areas such as computer vision, UAV navigation, and swarm intelligence. My work mainly centers on depth estimation, swarm simulation, and obstacle avoidance navigation, aiming to enable autonomous perception and collision avoidance for UAVs in complex dynamic environments.

Since 2015, I have completed my undergraduate, master’s, and doctoral studies at the School of Mechatronical Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology. I have published related research in journals and conferences, such as T-ASE, RA-L, and IROS and so on. I am currently actively seeking postdoctoral or research-oriented positions.

我的研究方向是无人机视觉感知及自主导航，方向涵盖计算机视觉、无人机导航及集群智能。 我的研究关注于深度估计、集群仿真、避障导航三方面，实现无人机在复杂动态环境下的自主感知和避障任务。

自 2015 年起至今，在北京理工大学机电学院完成本科、硕士和博士阶段的学习任务。在目前已经在T-ASE、RA-L、IROS等期刊会议发表相关研究成果。 目前正在积极寻求博士后或研究类职位。

Vision-Based Perception

End-to-End Learning
