PhD of BIT · School of Mechatronical Engineering 北京理工大学 · 机电学院博士研究生

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the School of Mechatronical Engineering, Beijing Institute of Technology. My research focuses on intelligent drone swarms, with interests spanning computer vision perception, end-to-end learning, autonomous obstacle avoidance for UAVs, and swarm intelligence simulation. My research goal is to enable vision-based cooperative UAV swarm missions in denied environments.

我目前在北京理工大学机电学院攻读博士学位。我的研究聚焦于智能无人机集群，研究兴趣涵盖计算机视觉感知、端到端学习、无人机自主避障以及集群智能仿真。我的研究目标是在拒止环境中实现基于视觉的无人机集群协同任务。

Since 2015, I have completed my bachelor's and master's degrees at Beijing Institute of Technology, and I also completed my doctoral coursework with outstanding academic performance. I expect to graduate by the end of 2026. At present, I am seeking a postdoctoral or research-related position.

自2015年起，我在北京理工大学完成了本科和硕士学业，博士课程成绩优异。预计于2026年底毕业。目前正在寻求博士后或科研相关职位。
